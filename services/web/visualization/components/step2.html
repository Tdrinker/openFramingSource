Decide Frames Inductively and Deductively

In Step 2, we recommend you consult the LDA topic modeling results from Step 1 (inductive) and previous literature about media framing of the issue of your interest (deductive), and then decide a list of frames to be analyzed. You may include both "generic frames" such as human interest, conflict, attribution of responsibility, and economic consequences, which can be applied to the analysis of a variety of issues, and "issue-specific frames," which are often used to discuss a particular issue. See our demo for more information about how to decide frames to be analyzed.
